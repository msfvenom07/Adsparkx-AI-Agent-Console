# Customer Refund Policy and Transit Times

This policy outlines guidelines for issuing, tracking, and completing customer refunds.

## Issuing Refunds

- Merchants can issue full or partial refunds directly via the dashboard or via the `/refunds` API endpoint.
- Refunds can only be sent back to the original card or bank account used for the purchase. Under no circumstances should support personnel process refunds to an alternate card or PayPal account.
- Original transaction fees (2.9% + $0.30) are not returned by credit card networks and are thus non-refundable.

## Customer Refund Timelines

- **Credit Cards**: Refunded amounts take 5 to 10 business days to appear on the customer's bank statement.
- **ACH / Direct Debit**: ACH refunds can take up to 7 business days to process and clear back to the bank account.
