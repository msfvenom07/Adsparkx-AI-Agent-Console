# Browser Cache and Cookie Management Guide

If you are experiencing loading issues, blank pages, or interface errors on the Adsparkx AI dashboard, clearing your browser cache and cookies can resolve the problem.

## How to Clear Cookies and Cache

### Google Chrome
1. Click the three dots menu in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Delete browsing data...** (or press `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`).
3. Choose a time range (e.g., **All time**).
4. Check the boxes next to **Cookies and other site data** and **Cached images and files**.
5. Click **Delete data**.

### Mozilla Firefox
1. Click the menu button and select **Settings**.
2. Select the **Privacy & Security** panel.
3. In the **Cookies and Site Data** section, click **Clear Data...**.
4. Check both boxes and click **Clear**.

### Safari (macOS)
1. Choose **Safari** > **Settings** (or **Preferences**) in the top menu bar.
2. Click the **Privacy** tab, then click **Manage Website Data...**.
3. Select **Remove All** and confirm.
